HOW TO BUY A FARM

* Go to the ultima farm webpage
* Scroll down the page to see the different packages
* Click on the down arrow in the selection box
Ultima Minters
From Light to Starter Minter
Ultima Minters + Cards
From Basic to Ultima Minter
* Select your Package
* Click on Upgrade
* Scroll down and click on your payment method
* Tick the box
* Click on Purchase
* Copy the address provided and send your desired coins from exchange of your choice
* Remain on the page till the payment is received
Once it's received, a congratulatory message will pop up
